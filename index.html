Data Usage & Privacy

We believe in transparency about how your data is used in our application.

Data We Collect and Why:

  • Schedule Data: Your lesson schedules, notes, and reminders are stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to your Google Drive account for data preservation.
  • Images: Photos you attach to lessons are stored locally on your device and included in Google Drive backups if you enable that feature.
  • App Preferences: Language settings and display preferences are stored locally to personalize your experience.
  • Google Account Information: When using Google Drive backup, we access only your email address to authenticate and store backup files in your personal Drive storage.

What We Don't Collect:

  • We do not collect personal identification information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes
  • We do not track your usage across other applications or websites

All data processing happens either locally on your device or through secure Google APIs when using backup features. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings.
